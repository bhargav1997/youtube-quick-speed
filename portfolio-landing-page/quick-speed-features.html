Why You Need
YouTube Quick Speed

A detailed breakdown of how each tool transforms your viewing experience.

The Problem

YouTube's Default Speed Limit

Standard players limit you to 2x speed. For fast readers and skimmers, this is often too slow. Navigating through menus to change speed breaks your flow.

The Solution

Precision Speed Control

We unlock speeds up to 16x. Whether you want to breeze through a lecture at 2.5x or analyze a sports play at 0.5x, you have total control via hotkeys (S / D) without ever touching your mouse.

The Need

Learning by Repetition

Trying to learn a guitar riff, a coding concept, or a dance move? Manually dragging the seeker bar back every 10 seconds is frustrating and inaccurate.

The Solution

A-B Loop System

Mark a start point (A) and an end point (B). The video will loop that specific section indefinitely. It's the ultimate tool for musicians, students, and self-learners.

The Issue

Quiet Audio Recordings

Some uploads are just too quiet, even at 100% system volume. This is especially problematic on laptops or in noisy environments.

The Solution

Volume Boost

Push beyond the limit. Our audio engine allows you to boost volume up to 600%, ensuring you never miss a word.

The Distraction

The Sidebar Trap

YouTube is designed to keep you clicking. Recommended videos, comments, and shorts are constantly fighting for your attention.

The Solution

Zen Mode

One click (Z) hides everything except the video player. No comments, no sidebar, no distractions. Just you and the content you came for.

Experience the Difference

Once you try it, you won't go back to the standard player.
