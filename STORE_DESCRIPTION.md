**1. Nuclear Ad Control**
Ads rely on wasting your time. We give it back.

-  **Auto-Skip**: Instantly clicks "Skip Ad" the millisecond it's possible.
-  **Ad Fast-Forward**: For unskippable ads, we automatically speed them up to **16x**, turning a 30-second spot into a 2-second blip.

**2. Dynamic "Boost" Mode**
Inspired by modern short-form video apps.

-  **Hold to Speed Up**: Simply hold `Shift` (or Ctrl/Alt) to temporarily boost speed to **2x, 3x, or even 5x**.
-  **Release to Normalize**: Let go to instantly return to normal speed. Perfect for breezing through boring intros or silence.

**3. Enhanced Playback Tools**

-  **Volume Booster**: Is the video too effortless? Crank the volume up to **500%**.
-  **Precision Speed**: Set any constant speed you want (e.g., 2.5x) specifically for the video.
-  **A-B Loop**: Isolate a specific part of the video and loop it indefinitely.
